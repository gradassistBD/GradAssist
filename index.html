The GradAssist Story

Founded by Researchers, For Researchers.

GradAssist was established by a cohort of PhD researchers in South Korea who recognized a critical gap in high-level academic mentorship.

We provide more than just document editing; we offer a Strategic Pedagogy. By aligning your unique academic background with the specific expectations of global Principal Investigators (PIs), we help you secure fully-funded positions in top-tier research laboratories.
